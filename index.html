I worked as a technical consultant for Fiori, where I troubleshooted system errors, deployed new applications to the dashboard, and worked with backend/security professionals to get applications working properly. I often worked remotely with a global team, but I occasionally traveled to client sites to do on-site work. I consulted on web applications with project owners, business leads, and UX designers to increase app efficiency and provide a better UX for the end user.
